Chocolate cake

Here is a fast and easy chocolate cake recipe! Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt chocolate and butter together.

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs, and flour.

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough.

4

 Bake in the oven.
